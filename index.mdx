## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.joincamino.com](https://app.joincamino.com) and set up your workspace with your company name and branding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your first template">
    Create an onboarding template for your most common role. Add tasks, resources, and introduce key team members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first employee">
    Enter a new hire's details or import them from your HRIS. Assign a template and set their start date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch it happen">
    Camino automatically sends the new hire their portal link, kicks off tasks, and notifies stakeholders — all on schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  Not sure where to start? Read the [Core Concepts](/core-concepts) page to understand how templates, tasks, and workflows fit together before diving into setup.
