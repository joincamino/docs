Welcome to Camino! Your onboarding portal is your personal home base for everything you need during your first days and weeks at your new company. It's entirely web-based, so there's nothing to download or install — just open a browser on any device and you're ready to go.

<Note>
  Everything you see in your portal — your tasks, resources, and introductions — was personalized for your specific role and team by your company's HR team. Your experience may look different from a colleague's, and that's by design.
</Note>

## Accessing Your Portal

You'll receive an email invite from Camino with a direct link to your portal. You can also access it at any time by visiting [app.joincamino.com](https://app.joincamino.com) and signing in with the credentials from your invite email.

<Tip>
  Bookmark your portal link right away so you can find it quickly throughout your onboarding. On mobile, you can add it to your home screen for even faster access.
</Tip>

### First-Time Portal Access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your invite email">
    Look for an email from Camino in your inbox (check your personal email if you haven't started yet, or your work email once your account is set up). Click the **Get Started** button in the email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your password">
    On your first visit, you'll be prompted to create a password. Choose something secure and save it somewhere safe — you'll use it every time you log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add a profile photo and fill in any basic info requested, such as your preferred name or pronouns. This helps your teammates recognize you right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your portal home page">
    Once you're in, take a moment to look around. You'll find your welcome message, a snapshot of your tasks, and a few quick links to get you oriented.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's on Your Portal Home Page

Your portal home page gives you a bird's-eye view of your entire onboarding journey. Here's what you'll find when you log in:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Welcome Message" icon="hand-wave">
    A personal note from your manager or the company welcoming you to the team. This is a great place to start on your first visit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Progress Overview" icon="chart-line">
    A progress indicator showing how many tasks you've completed out of your total — so you always know where you stand in your onboarding journey.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Upcoming Tasks" icon="calendar-check">
    A snapshot of tasks due soon, so you can stay on top of what needs your attention without digging through the full task list.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Introductions" icon="users">
    Meet your manager, onboarding buddy, and teammates right from the home page. Profiles include roles, fun facts, and contact links.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quick Links" icon="link">
    Shortcuts to important resources — like your employee handbook, IT setup guides, or benefit enrollment forms — curated by your HR team.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Preboarding: Getting a Head Start

If your company has enabled preboarding, you can access your portal before your official start date. This lets you review introductory content, complete early tasks like paperwork, and get familiar with your company before day one. You'll see a **Preboarding** section in your task list with everything that's available ahead of your start date.

Preboarding tasks are a great way to hit the ground running — but don't worry if you'd rather wait until your first day. Nothing is lost if you log in later.

## Updating Your Profile

Your profile photo and basic information help your new teammates put a face to your name. To update your profile:

1. Click your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of the portal.
2. Select **Edit Profile** from the dropdown.
3. Upload a photo, update your display name, and fill in any other fields your company has set up.
4. Click **Save** to apply your changes.

## Mobile Access

Your Camino portal is fully mobile-friendly and works on any smartphone or tablet browser — no app download required. Everything you can do on a desktop, you can do on your phone: check your task list, mark tasks complete, read resources, and browse team profiles. Just navigate to [app.joincamino.com](https://app.joincamino.com) in your mobile browser and log in as usual.
