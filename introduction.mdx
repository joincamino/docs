Camino is a modern employee onboarding platform built around a simple belief: every new hire deserves an experience tailored to who they are and what they need. Instead of sending a generic checklist and hoping for the best, Camino gives your People team the tools to design structured, personalized onboarding journeys — complete with automated workflows, a branded new hire portal, and real-time progress tracking — so no one slips through the cracks.

## Who Camino Is For

Camino serves two groups of people, and it's designed to delight both of them.

**HR admins and People teams** use Camino to build and manage onboarding programs. You create templates, define tasks, set up automated workflows, connect your existing tools, and monitor how every new hire is progressing — all from a single dashboard.

**New hires** experience Camino through a personalized portal that greets them before their first day and guides them through everything they need to do, know, and read in their first weeks on the job.

<Note>
  Camino operates from two perspectives: the **admin view**, where your People team builds and manages onboarding programs, and the **new hire portal**, where employees experience their personalized journey. This documentation covers both — look for labels indicating which perspective each guide applies to.
</Note>

## What You Can Do with Camino

Camino bundles everything your team needs to run a best-in-class onboarding program into one platform.

- **Onboarding Templates** — Build reusable onboarding blueprints tailored to specific roles, teams, or locations. Assign them to new hires in seconds.
- **Task Checklists** — Create structured to-do lists for new hires, managers, IT, and any other stakeholder involved in the onboarding process.
- **Automated Workflows** — Trigger actions automatically based on events like a hire's start date, task completion, or a 30-day milestone — no manual follow-up required.
- **New Hire Portal** — Give every employee a branded, personalized web portal with their tasks, resources, team introductions, and company information, ready before day one.
- **Team Introductions** — Help new hires put faces to names with structured introductions to teammates, managers, and onboarding buddies.
- **Integrations** — Connect Camino to your HRIS, Slack, and Google Workspace to sync employee data and deliver onboarding content where your team already works.
- **Progress Tracking Dashboards** — See at a glance which new hires are on track, which tasks are overdue, and where your onboarding program has room to improve.
